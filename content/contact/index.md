## What to Include in Your Enquiry

To help us provide an accurate quote and timeline, please include:

1. **Project Description:** Brief overview of what you're trying to achieve
2. **Technical Requirements:** Any specific technical constraints or requirements
3. **Timeline:** Your desired completion date or project timeline
4. **Budget:** Approximate budget range (helps us suggest the best approach)
5. **Industry/Application:** What industry or application this is for
6. **Current Status:** Are you starting from scratch or enhancing an existing system?
