## How Pricing Works

All PCB design packages are quoted based on:
- Complexity of the circuit design
- Number of layers required
- Component count and selection
- Testing requirements
- Timeline and delivery urgency

**Get a detailed quote tailored to your project** by [contacting us](/contact/) with your requirements.
