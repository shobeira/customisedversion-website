## What This Looks Like in Practice

These scenarios illustrate the kind of problems CustomisedVersion solves. Each is representative of a typical engagement.

### A Product Idea Becomes Hardware

**The situation:** A commercial fisherman has an idea for a man-overboard safety device that uses long-range radio to alert the crew. He knows what it should do but has no electronics background.

**What CustomisedVersion does:** Engineers the complete electronics — power supply design for marine input voltages, microcontroller and radio module selection, multi-GNSS positioning, PCB schematic and layout, component sourcing, prototype assembly and testing. The design moves through defined stages: proof of concept first, then manufacturing-ready files when the prototype is validated.

**The outcome:** A tested, working prototype with a clear path to manufacturing. The product owner controls when and how to move to production.

---

### Aquaculture Monitoring That Doesn't Sleep

**The situation:** An aquaculture operator needs continuous dissolved oxygen monitoring with automated alerts — manual spot-checks miss critical overnight events, and EPA compliance requires documented records.

**What CustomisedVersion does:** Designs a continuous monitoring system with data logging, SMS alerts when DO drops below safe thresholds, and automatic compliance documentation. Sensors, communication, and power are engineered for the specific site conditions.

**The outcome:** Stock protected around the clock. Compliance records generated automatically. Manual monitoring time reduced significantly.

---

### A Smart Home That Actually Fits the Home

**The situation:** A homeowner wants climate control, lighting automation, and energy monitoring tailored to their specific property — not a generic off-the-shelf hub that half-works with their existing systems.

**What CustomisedVersion does:** Designs a custom controller that integrates the specific sensors, actuators, and protocols the property needs. The system is engineered for the actual space, not adapted from a product designed for a different one.

**The outcome:** Automation that works reliably for the specific property, with no compromises forced by off-the-shelf product limitations.

---

### An Engineering Team Needs a Specialist Board

**The situation:** A product company is developing a new device with LoRa communication and GNSS positioning, but the in-house team's strength is firmware, not RF-adjacent PCB layout. They need the board designed to EMC compliance standards while their team focuses on the application software.

**What CustomisedVersion does:** Takes the functional specification and delivers a production-ready PCB design — schematic, layout, BOM, Gerber files — engineered to meet EN55032 Class B requirements. Design reviews and iteration happen within the client's existing workflow.

**The outcome:** A manufacturable board delivered on schedule, designed by a specialist, while the internal team stays focused on their core work.

---

### Frost Protection Without the 2 am Alarm

**The situation:** A vineyard operator is setting alarms for 2 am manual frost checks. One missed night can destroy an entire season's crop.

**What CustomisedVersion does:** Designs a sensor network with automated frost fan activation when temperatures drop below the critical threshold. SMS confirmation of activation so the operator knows the system responded without getting out of bed.

**The outcome:** Frost fans activate automatically. One saved season pays for the system.
